SERVICE

Maintain

Vehicles purchased from our company are guaranteed for 5 years. As long as the damage is not caused by human factors, free repair and replacement of parts are provided. For details, you can contact us through the chip network. As long as it is within our service scope, we will dispatch service personnel to your location for evaluation and repair.

Neurous Setting

The smart phone network developed by our company uses the combination of the mobile phone and the computer provided by the computer, which can make the control have an automatic mode that conforms to the main control, and realize the operation through the connection between the human-machine control and the vehicle.

Aerial Refueling

Air vehicles do not need to worry about the problem of no fuel in the middle. As long as the chip network and automatic detection system are used, you can reserve the nearest drone to your location.
