In the distant future, huge environmental changes have taken place in the space where people live. The dark city and disordered government, accompanied by the endless sound of rain, echo the never-ending human being drowned in conflict, chaos and disorder cycle...

In a chaotic world, people have left the earth to explore a better living environment. The rest must struggle desperately in this chaotic world. In such a turbulent situation, FUTURE is responsible for providing excellent vehicles to protect their own safety.

FUTURE company is classified in Megacorporation, mainly to provide excellent vehicles. Whether it is a land armored vehicle, an unmanned vehicle or a flying vehicle, it should be fully equipped. In the future, it will expand to the universe, allowing people to escape from this gradually destroyed planet as soon as possible.
