SPACE STATION

The space relay station set up by our company on the other side of the galaxy is made of special metals found on Mars. Currently in the final setup stage, with other space stations capable of accommodating nearly 100,000 people, the space station has facilities such as relay stations, shopping malls, food courts, sports fields, etc., and has been opened for migration.

The company has set up relay stations in many places in outer space to facilitate the relocation of human beings. It updates the information and dynamics of the first universe every day.

There is also a professional space exploration team in the relay station cabin to record the status and detection of each planet, find more useful resources and discover new creatures.

There are also a variety of entertainment equipment and services in the cabin, as well as automated planting rooms and automated kitchens, providing organic crops.
